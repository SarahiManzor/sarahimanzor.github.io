Cannon Craze

About this project

Start of Development: Mid July 2015

End of Development: Early September 2015

Total lines of code: 3630

Estimated time spent on development: ~200 hours

Description: The goal of this game is to launch yourself as far as you can. Every round you play rewards you with money which you can spend on upgrades to your equipment. There are also many obstacles along the way that can help you or slow you down.

Working on this project helped enhance my problem solving and analytical skills in Unity.
